What I do

I am a 2D Artist, Graphic Designer and Web Developer who uses prior experiences to merge my skills from areas of tech and design. My skills such as Adobe Creative Suite, Procreate, and Adobe InDesign, from design and my skills such as HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap acquired from tech allows me to perform and execute task in a unique way that can incorporate vision and needs into results. As a Designer and Educator, I always pursued the dream of seeing the media industry as an inclusive place where people like me can build a career; a place where the once oppressed can tell their stories, relate to their own role models and finally open gates of diversity to everybody else. This is the diversity and inclusion approach I wish to bring to the gaming and web development industry. I am currently employed as adjunct instructor for the Games and Animation BFA at the University of Cincinnati.

HOW

I DESIGN IMAGES and MEDIA using LudoPoetics, a method I developed based on Roland Barthes Semiotics to instigate critical thinking. My tools are Adobe Creative Suite, Procreate, HTML/ CSS/ Bootstrap, JavaScript, Google Apps and Minecraft. My GitHub

WHAT FOR

I TEACH using a didatic approach inspired by bell hooks: rescuing the traditional knowledge of the original peoples as a means to contribute to the continuity of society, transgressing and then improving it. My tools are Google Apps, Canvas, Wordpress and Microsoft Office.

WHY

I RESEARCH the potential of narrative games as poetic expression and means of changing the world by addressing diversity and representation inspired by Lindsay Grace. My tools are Decolonial Studies, Semiotics, participant observation and discourse analysis.
